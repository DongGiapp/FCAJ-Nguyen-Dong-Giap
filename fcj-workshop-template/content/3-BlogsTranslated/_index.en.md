During the internship, I translated and summarized the following AWS-related blogs:

### [Blog 1 - Optimizing LLM costs with Intelligent Prompt Routing on Amazon Bedrock](3.1-Blog1/)

This article by Gerardo Arroyo (AWS Community Builder) explains **Intelligent Prompt Routing** on Amazon Bedrock, a mechanism that automatically routes prompts to the most suitable model, such as Haiku for simpler tasks and Sonnet for more complex tasks. The translated blog includes cost analysis, applications for Model Agent architecture, and limitations to consider in production.

### [Blog 2 - Optimizing backend costs with Serverless architecture and AWS Lambda](3.2-Blog2/)

This article analyzes the cost problem of traditional EC2-based backend architecture, especially idle cost, and how **AWS Lambda** addresses it through a pay-per-use model. It covers the nature of FaaS, EC2 vs Lambda cost comparison, AI Agent and chatbot use cases with Bedrock, event-driven architecture, and limitations such as cold start.

### [Blog 3 - Why Serverless is the future: Benefits of AWS Lambda](3.3-Blog3/)

This article explains the nature of Serverless and the key benefits of AWS Lambda, including pay-as-you-go pricing, automatic scaling, reduced server administration, and faster product development. It also covers practical use cases such as image/video processing, REST APIs, cron jobs, and real-time data processing, while noting limitations such as cold start and execution time limits.
