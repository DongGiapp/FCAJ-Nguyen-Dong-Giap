# FCAJ Community Day

**FCAJ Community Day** was an AWS First Cloud AI Journey community event organized by Huynh Hoang Long, Thien Lu, and Tran Dai Vi. The event focused on AI, cloud architecture, Amazon CloudFront, Amazon Quick Suite, LLM behavior, and multi-agent systems in practical scenarios.

## Key Takeaways

### Thinking about AI context

The session about context showed that AI output quality depends not only on the model, but also on the surrounding information provided to it. Better prompts, memory, and relevant context help AI produce more accurate and useful responses.

### Cloud architecture perspective

The CloudFront session helped me understand the role of the edge layer in improving performance, security, reliability, and cost optimization. This is useful knowledge for designing web systems or APIs with distributed users.

### AI for data and workflows

The Amazon Quick sessions showed how AI can help non-technical users explore data, create dashboards, build workflows, and collaborate through natural language.

### Product-building lessons

The LotusHacks and UTMorpho sharing highlighted the pressure of building a product in a short time, from defining the problem and choosing a solution to delivering a demo and learning from the experience.

### Practical awareness of LLMs and multi-agent systems

The sessions on non-determinism and multi-agent systems showed that production AI systems require more than good prompts. Teams must consider stability, guardrails, compliance, agent responsibilities, and ROI measurement.
